About

This is the Purchase Request System project that was completed at MAX Technical Training during the .NET bootcamp. The purpose of this project was to provide the aspiring developers with the opportunity to develop a full-stack project (database, back-end, front-end).


This particular version of the project was developed around a scenario in which an individual at a business needs to purchase items needed for the business to run. A user may submit a request to purchase an item or set of items, and then an individual with "reviewer" rights (who is not the same invidual as the initial user), may either approve or reject the request.
